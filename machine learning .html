April Python Pune Meetup(@PythonPune)

Python pune meetup was orgnaised on April 30,2016 at Redhat, pune. In python pune meetUp there was around 70 to 75 peolpe has join.

Python pune meetup was organised by Mr. Chandan kumar at beging of machine learning meetup chandan introduce to speaker Satish Patil (@DataGeekSatish) and Sudarshan Gadhave (@sudarshan1989) who took a session on Machine Learning.

The meetup was started around 10:45 with the introduction where everybody introduced themselves. Almost everybody knew python, few people who were experience in machine learning and few people where completely new to Machine Learning.

Satish Partil is the founder and Chief Data Scientist of Lemoxo Technologies, Pune where he advise companies large and small on their data strategy. he has 10+ year of research in the field of drug discovery and development. He told a few real life machine learning examples from his field in the meetup.

Satish is passionate about applying technology, artificial intelligence, design thinking and cognitive science to better understand, predict and improve business functions. He has a great interest in Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, Data Visualisation, Big Data.

Satish Patil has covered the following topics which are listed below

  1. What is Machine Learning
  2. The Black Box of Machine Learning
  3. features
  4. training and test data set
  5. classification
  6. clustering
  7. pure and impure states
  8. decision tree
  9. supervised and unsupervised learning
  10. market basket analysis
  11. data pre-processing
  12. Titanic data set
  13. K means algorithm
